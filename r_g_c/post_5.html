A game which satisfies the following constraints. 2 players place must take turns placing differently colored stones on a board the stones are two-sided, and some in-game event(s) cause stones to flip (win condition needn't be Othello win condition) This is nice and easy, and no doubt there's plenty of potential to design something preferable to Othello. Another challenge, and one for which Christian's game would qualify, would be a pattern-building game where there are multiple local- pattern win conditions. If you guys are agreeable to one of these, let me know. I'll choose the one that gets more interest in this thread and then I'll announce a deadline, say 3 weeks or a month later, anybody who wants can post an entry here in our new little forum, and then we'll use (condorcet?) voting to determine the winner.
