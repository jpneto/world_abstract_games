We are also testing another game idea on the same board, with the same Y-goal, but with a different way to try to de-emphasize the centre. The idea is to make the more central cells more valuable, by assigning them "costs", typically a number from 1 to 3; 3 in the centre rings, 1 on the outside rings. Then, a turn is to place 1, 2, 3 or 4 stones, on cells with total value 4. There are a few other wrinkles too.
