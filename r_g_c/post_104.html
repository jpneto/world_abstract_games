Hi, I don't think this is very famous in backgammon circles, because it is a very unlikely possibility. However the maximum number of pieces on the bar (i.e. captured) is 15 and it happens when you capture all the pieces of the opponent. Of course it can happen that there are pieces of both players on the bar, but the total number can not increase. This is because you can only capture by reentering (if you have some piece on the bar you can't move other ones) so you will capture at most one opponent piece for each of yours which leave the bar.
