Details about the competition can be found here: https://github.com/Ludeme/LudiiAICompetition The Ludii AI Competition is a General Game Playing competition focussed on developing agents that can play a wide variety of board games. The events will use Ludii (https://ludii.games/), a general game system, to provide the necessary games and API. Games will be provided in the Ludii (*.lud) game description format. The version used for this competition (1.3.2) of Ludii includes over 1,000 games. How to Register? To participate in any of the tracks, please send us an email at ludii...@gmail.com to register. The required information is your first and last name, the name of your agent, and an email to contact you. Registration is free. This competition is also a good opportunity for students (PhD, Master or Bachelor) to experiment with AI techniques and apply them to games. So please, if you know anyone who might be interested, spread this announcement. The three competition events are: 1. Kilothon: Best utility obtained on more than 1,000 games against UCT.
