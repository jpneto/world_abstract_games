Like reversi, except that you flip over sequences of both your opponent's colour and the neutral ones. The latter turn to your own colour, the former, to neutral. Quick implementation here: http://87.119.183.129/perl/Preversi/PreversiCgi.pl?action=newgame (The reversi engine it's based on is pretty poor and buggy, but I've fixed it up enough that if you click randomly you might lose.
