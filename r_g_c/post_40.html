Hi, playing around with Luis' Verto Zillions implementation (which is very nice), especially with the variant "Positional Verto" turned on, I found that compulsory captures are something the game actually doesn't need. In fact, the positional variant is the better game. I thought about this variant before, but somehow the Reversi/Checkers scheme of compulsion forced me into that direction. Okay, this won't affect the contest, the now old, submitted version is object of your evaluation. But beyond that, the standard rules will now go *without* compulsory captures. It is still mandatory to make all possible captures, but you still won't have to follow the maximum path. I'll change the Applet soon. Have a nice day, Dieter
