I like the design of Catchup very much, but I do see one thing that might improve it. The "second-largest group" tiebreak is, in my opinion, artificial and distracting. I recommend that a komi of .5 be given to the second player. That way, the first player needs to outscore his opponent by one or more to win, and the second player needs to score evenly with his opponent or better to win. This resolves ties in a way that it pretty fair (since there is, presumably, still somewhat of a first-player advantage despite the catchup mechanism). It also removes the distraction of considering a second largest group and keeps the players' focus on the largest group. Steve
