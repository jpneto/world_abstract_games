The strategies are that of an inverted Hex though, avoid creating bridges, play from the edge inward. The problem is definitely a lack of clarity, which can be expected, given that Hex involves a lot of reading ahead. All of a sudden you have to consider all the possible ways to avoid connection instead of the few ways you will be able to achieve it. Another annoying thing is the inevitable Nim situations, where both players will waste moves trying to avoid playing particular cells. These become more and more frequent with greater skill.
