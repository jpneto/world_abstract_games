To spice things up and out of curiosity, here's a question, of the desert island variety: Among games which 1. ...were invented this century; and 2. ...were not invented by you, Which is your desert island game? For me it would be Slither (aka Particle Bond). I can't believe it hasn't gotten more acclaim. All the formal beauty of Hex, but the connections break and reform in a totally arresting way as the game proceeds, leading to sudden changes of fortune and impossibly delightful tactics. No description can do it justice though. You have to play it to see. There are similar connections games on Hex boards, but they're not nearly as good as this square board game. The square board and orthogonal-only connections are key.

No single runner up comes even close. It is a breakthrough design with amazing gameplay - one of the best games in history. If "this century" is referring to 21st century, then it is not clear whether 2000 is part of the 20th century or the 21st. An old German law says that the century starts with the 00 year.
