Hi Nick, Great work on compiling the guide for online game resources, that's potentially very useful. ================================================== These might be worth adding: 1. The Games Journal: http://www.thegamesjournal.com This is an online archive of articles about abstract games, all freely available. It hasn't been updated since 2005 but contains some of the best writing on games, including Thompson's classic "Defining the Abstract". 2. Lost Treasures from Games & Puzzles: https://rapidshare.com/#!download|817p3|383774785|Lost_Treasures_from_Games___Puzzles.pdf|33085|0|0|1|referer-9C3C4EE35D7D7BD05AECE02C5AF90FA4 This is a (large) .pdf that contains photocopies of the more interesting pages of the Games & Puzzles magazine from the 1970s, compiled by Fred Horn. It contains 167 pages of new game ideas, great fun to flip through, just goes to show that there isn't much that hasn't been discovered before. A lot of recent games are probably rediscoveries of ideas included in this collection. ================================================== You might also consider a link to the game-related pages at the US Patent Office site, where you can find interesting things that authors may not otherwise publicly announce. I had fun trawling through the "Games: Path forming" patents looking for things to include in the connection games book. It's also fascinating to see how the USPO split the games class into its various sub-categories, giving a very practical classification scheme for games. Comparing the (practical) USPO classification to existing (theoretical) classifications could be an interesting little side-project in itself sometime. Class 273: Amusement Devices: Games http://www.uspto.gov/web/patents/classification/uspc273/sched273.htm Schedule 236: Board Games, Pieces, or Boards Therefor http://www.uspto.gov/web/patents/classification/uspc273/defs273.htm#C273S236000 Schedule 292: Card or Tile Game, Cards or Tiles Therefor http://www.uspto.gov/web/patents/classification/uspc273/defs273.htm#C273S292000 Schedule 148R: Games Accessories: http://www.uspto.gov/web/patents/classification/uspc273/defs273.htm#C273S148000 Schedule 153R: Puzzles: http://www.uspto.gov/web/patents/classification/uspc273/defs273.htm#C273S153000 ================================================== These fora may or may not be relevant: 1.
