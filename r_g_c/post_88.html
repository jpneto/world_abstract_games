Hi, The Challenge is now over and the prize winners have been decided - congratulations Matt Green! There was a good turnout of 45 games from 22 designers, the best of which are described in the Shibumi Rule Book: http://www.lulu.com/product/paperback/shibumi-rule-book/18940123 This is a full-colour, professionally bound book describing the system and the best Shibumi games so far, including the Challenge prize winners and several other of our favourite entries. You can also download the rule book for free as a low resolution .pdf. This is suitable for browsing on small screens, but the printed version is much nicer: http://www.mogal.ai/shibumi/a5-shibumi-v1.1-low.pdf Please keep submitting new game ideas, they might make it into the next edition.
