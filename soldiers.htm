Games of Soldiers

 

This page presents games that are played with Go stones using a regular square or hexagonal board, for two players and played in sequential turns. Most games here are not related to Go, since there is a specific section for Go variants at this site.

Among board game sites, the most comprehensive is Board Game Geek (the IMDB of board games). Another options to find old rules are François Haffner's JeuxSoc, Jose Diaz's Abstract Strategy Games, James Master's Traditional Board Games, Justin du Coeur's Period Games, Bruce Whitehill's The Big Game Hunter, Mats Winther's Board Games and GARD - GAmes Research Database.

To play online check Abstract Play, IG Game Center, and BoardSpace. To find a computer opponent, check the meta-game engines of Zillions, AiAi, and Ludii.

Here are the actual games included in this list (335 so far, not including every variant). Each game has a set of icons (position the mouse over them) and a short description to give an instant feeling of the game playability. To know more about each game, just click on the .
