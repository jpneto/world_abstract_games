GAMES
from Sid Sackson's Diaries

1963-1973

Sid Sackson, the famous game designer, wrote a game-related diary between 1963 and 1997. Among several types of activities, he regularly describes games he saw, or his own game ideas that were (or not) developed later. This page shows some of the entries related to abstract board games.
