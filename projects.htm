Game Projects


This page includes several game-related projects regarding abstract games, namely:

  • several software applications for visualizing games (Javascript, Postscript, or Python)
  • a proposal for a gaming set to play board games, in the sense that a deck of cards is used to play card games
  • the backup of most posts published in the defunct rec.games.abstract (1992-2018) and Recre.Games.Combinatorial (2010-2014) newsgroups
    • it is possible to search the posts' titles; for better search go to Google Groups (note: at Google, posts might be truncated)
  • the backup of all posts published in the defunct Yahoo! Abstract Games club (2000-2011)
  • all the editions of the Portuguese Board Games Contest, an annual event where thousands of students gather to play abstract games
