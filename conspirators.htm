CONSPIRATEURS

Traditional, late XVIIIth Century France

Conspirators can be played on a Go board, with the following setup:


  • FIRST PHASE [DROP] - Both players take turns to drop their 20 stones (of a total of 40 stones) on some available empty cells inside the green area.
  • SECOND PHASE [MOVE] - At each turn, each player move one stone. A stone can move to one adjacent (orthogonal or diagonal) empty cell, or jump a stone of any color to the opposite empty cell, if other jumps are available, the stone can continue to jump (just like in Halma) in any direction.
    • The stones cannot jump outside the 17x17 area limited by the shelters (the 39 red dots on the board)
  • GOAL - A player wins when he places all of his stones inside the shelters.
