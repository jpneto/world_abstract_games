A Timeline for Abstract Games

About this page

This is a timeline covering the last 250 years regarding abstract games.

The list includes several categories:

  • book/article - relevant books (focusing on multiple games) and articles in this field
  • event - venues, contests, institutions, cultural moments regarding abstract games
  • games - a selection of good or relevant abstract games
  • ludeme - year of introduction of relevant game rule concepts
  • publisher - either very big game companies, or small companies very focused on abstract games
  • software - advances or important moments in board game programming
  • website - online resources

of course, every 'the first' sentence is open to change if new evidence appears
