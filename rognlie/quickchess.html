Rules of QuickChess

The QuickChess Home Page https://www.chessvariants.com/small.dir/quick.html contains a full description of the QuickChess variant, but here are the highlights...

Quick Chess was invented by Joe Miccio in 1991. It is played on a 5x6 board. The initial position is shown below. 

              a   b   c   d   e  
            +---+---+---+---+---+    Black
          6 |*N*|*Q*|*K*|*B*|*R*|  6
            +---+---+---+---+---+
          5 |*P*|*P*|*P*|*P*|*P*|  5
            +---+---+---+---+---+
          4 |   |:::|   |:::|   |  4
            +---+---+---+---+---+
          3 |:::|   |:::|   |:::|  3
            +---+---+---+---+---+
          2 | P |:P:| P |:P:| P |  2
            +---+---+---+---+---+
          1 |:N:| Q |:K:| B |:R:|  1
            +---+---+---+---+---+    White
              a   b   c   d   e

The rules are the same as standard chess, except:

  1. Pawns can only advance 1 space on the opening. Therefore, no En Passant capturing.
  2. Pawns may only promote to a captured piece.
  3. There is no castling.
