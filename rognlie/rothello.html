Introduction

Welcome to the network Reverse-Othello server.

The commands are the same for all pbmserv games.

Reverse-Othello is played with the same rules as Othello, except that you are trying to end of with the LEAST number of discs of your color at the end of the game. In other words, you are trying to lose a normal game of Othello. See the Othello help page for the complete rules of Othello.
