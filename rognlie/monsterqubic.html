The Rules of MonsterQubic

Object of the Game

Game Based on idea by David Bush.

On a 6x6x6 grid, two players alternate placing their pieces, attempting to form five (or six) in a row in any direction.

Movement Rules

On your turn, you place a piece of your colour wherever you wish.

The first player to go, may place his first piece only on the "surface" of the cube, excluding the corners.
