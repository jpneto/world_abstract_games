Rules of DoubleMoveChess All rules from normal chess apply, except as explained below.

  • Every turn (except white's first turn) consists of two normal chess moves. White only makes one move on his first turn to prevent him from having a large first move advantage.
  • Check and Checkmate rules are not used. To win, one must capture the opponent's king.
  • There is no enpassant capture.
