Rules of Go-Moku

Object of the Game

On a 15x15 board, each player tries to create a line of 5 (or more) pieces of their colour while trying to prevent their opponent from doing the same thing.

Movement Rules

On your turn, you place a piece of your colour wherever you desire on the board.
