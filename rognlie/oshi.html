Red goes first. Pieces move horizontally or vertically in a single direction up to as many spaces as they have tiers. 
Therefore, the 1-tier pieces move up to one space, a 2-tier piece moves up to 2 spaces, and so on. 
They can additionally push as many pieces as they have tiers. You can push your opponent's pieces, your own pieces, or a combination. 
You also cannot move a piece to the same spot it occupied the turn before.
First to push off 7 points wins. A 3-tier counts as 3 points, a 2-tier counts as 2 points and a 1-tier counts as a single point.
