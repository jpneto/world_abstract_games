Game Rules

  • Goal: Have the highest score when both players run out of pieces.
  • Scoring: 1 point for every piece on the board, PLUS 1 point for every empty hex that is adjacent ONLY to your pieces.
  • 🌺 Carnivore (6): At the end of a turn, the active player's carnivores destroy enemy pieces touching 2+ of them. Surviving enemy carnivores then retaliate.
  • 🌪️ Wind (6): When placed next to a piece, it must push that piece away in a straight line until it hits another piece or the edge.
  • 🌻 Throwing (6): When placed, move any adjacent piece into another empty space adjacent to the Throwing flower.
  • 🌼 Dandelion (3): Place on an empty hex, then select an adjacent piece (friendly or enemy, excluding Dandelions) to return it to its owner's reserve. Ko Rule: An enemy piece returned this way cannot be immediately placed back on the exact same hex on their next turn.
