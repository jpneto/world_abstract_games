Games of Kings and Soldiers

 

This section covers abstract board games with two set of pieces on each army, usually called the soldier and the King (but names may change...) and, as in the games of Soldiers section, these games are also played on a regular square or hexagonal board, for two players and played in sequential turns.

Here are the actual games included in this list (175 so far). Each game has a set of icons (position the mouse over them) and a short description to give an instant feeling of the game playability. To know more about each game, just click on the .
