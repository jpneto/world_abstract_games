Taylor - Neto games

João Pedro Neto
2005

João Pedro Neto (me) and Bill Taylor maintained an regular email conversation from c.1996 to 2021 (when Bill passed away).

After Bill's passing, I have made an effort to collect and organize all remaining emails from this period in a ~14000 page email archive. This archive includes many dozens of new games and variants, developed and playtested by us, that were never published. I decided to start re-reading the emails, more or less in chronological order, to salvage these games from oblivion, and present them in a curated catalogue, as a hommage to our friendship, and as a contribution to the online community of abstract games.
