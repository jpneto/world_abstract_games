As most of you know, The World of Abstract Games (WAG) website
consists of a large collection of abstract games divides into 4 main
sections: Go variants / Games of Soldiers / Games of King and
Soldiers / Games of Towers

At this point, my stock of known games yet to publish in WAG is
becoming thin (I still have ~20, but that's just 3 months). I want to
ask you guys the following:

- Check in your archives if you have some more game rules fitting
the criteria mentioned above (and send them to me, if you have any)

- Check the Unknown game section
(http://www.di.fc.ul.pt/~jpn/gv/unknown.htm) and see if you know any
of those games

- What do you think of a new section? I mean, what would be the
focus of a new group of games? I have one sugestion: puzzles (ie,
games for one player) played on a Go board. Do you think it would be
interesting? More ideas?
