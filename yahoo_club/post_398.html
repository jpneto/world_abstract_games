> I'm not clear on what you mean by "move is determined by the numeric
> sequence". Does this mean that piece 1 must be moved first,
followed by the
> next higher surviving piece on the player's next move, etc.?

If the players are Red and Blue, then Red drops 1, then Blue drops 1,
then Red drops 2, then Blue drops 2, (etc.), and after Blue drops 6,
then Red relocates 1, then Blue relocates 1, then Red relocates 2,
then Blue relocates 2, etc.

The original pieces, according to the review, were not numbered:
the "1" was a tile showing a circle, the "2" was an oval, and "3" -
"6" were regular polygons with that number of sides. (So think of a
circle as 1-sided, oval as 2-sided. Probably the oval had pointed
ends.)
