Also, while I'm writing, I should mention that in the summer of 2001
when I first wrote about Jade in this spot, I proposed that each
player play BOTH a White and Black stone each turn. It was Claude
Chaunier who then suggested the current rule, which is that each
player may play EITHER a White or Black stone each turn. Claude's
rule is the one I remembered when I recently dredged it up from
memory and wrote to Cameron about it, and I now think it makes more
sense.
