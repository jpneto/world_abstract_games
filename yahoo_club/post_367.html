There is a new game out there, Arimaa.
Anybody heard of it? The authors are a computer
scientist and his very young son, they made
a simple and funny game from a Chess set, I
love it. It's going to be tough to make a
computer opponent. There may be as many as
10 000 valid moves, although the game looks
easier than Go to humans. It's not the main
interest of this game, but they challenge
the world with a prize for an A.I. better
than humans before the next 20 years. I'll
try! Anyway, the game is rich and balanced
and yield some surprises :-)
