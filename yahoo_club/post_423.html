I have been playing some email and face2face games
and I think Jade is in a very good position to be a
remarkable game.

Based on my small amount of games so far, each player
"chooses" a colour and use it thru the initial phase.
But then the pressure starts to build up, since both
players start to create positions where it may be
advantageous to switch colour. That interlude is
very fun and may provoke a sequence of transitions
between the two players.

I had one game position where I would win the game,
no matter what colour was chosen by the opponent to
occupy the critical cell. A true zugzwang!
