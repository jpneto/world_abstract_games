Sign up now for Chess variant tournament

Author: fduniho

Date: 2004-03-05 00:38:07

Post ID: 544

Chessvariants.com will soon be hosting a multivariant Chess variant
tournament with the web-based PBM system, Game Courier. The deadline
for signing up is March 15, less than two weeks away. Each participant
in the tournament will play 6 to 11 games, depending on how many
people sign up, chosen from a list of 9 to 14 games. These games were
chosen for the tournament by two polls conducted earlier this year and
last year. The top 14 games, from which the games in this tournament
may be chosen, include 7 classic games described in Pritchard's
Encyclopedia of Chess Variants and 7 more recent games. Details on the
tournament can be found at:

http://play.chessvariants.com/tournaments/gamecourier1.html

You will also be able to sign up from the same page.
