The absolute record of Morpion solitaire is 170. There are 3 records
of 170, found by different people. They all date from 1982, and they
still stand. (For records the new points should be replaced by
numbers. It is then easy to follow the score and check if it is
correct.) If the game will ever stop was an open question. Some people
from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology picked the game up from
my book, and wrote a paper about it. They seem to have proven that the
puzzle will stop, which was to be expected. See:
http://theory.lcs.mit.edu/~edemaine/papers/Morpion_FUN2004/. Or other
pages of Erik Demaine. (I don't understand much about it,since I am
not a mathematician). See also: http://euler.free.fr/morpion.htm. Much
more is to be said about this puzzle: you can for example free the
original 36 points from their arrangement in a cross. Bigger records
are then possible.
