A few abstract card games (perfect information, no luck, turn based,
two parties):

1. Nim-like Games:
- Abstrac
Author: David Parlett
Source: http://www.davidparlett.co.uk/oricards/abstrac.html
- Suit Yourself
Author: Sid Sackson
Source: Sid Sackson. A Gamut of Games. 1992, 92-97.

2. Mate-giving Games:
- Matt (in English: "Mate")
Author: G. Capellen
Sources: Sid Sackson. A Gamut of Games. 1992, 2-7.
G. Capellen. Zwei neue Kriegsspiele. 1915.

3. Card Games on imaginary boards:
- Pattern
Author: Sid Sackson
Source: Sid Sackson. A Gamut of Games. 1992, 86-91.
- Netak
Author: Ralf Gering
Source: personal communication
