Tumblingdown is a perfect abstract by many measures - it's
extremely simple, with vigorous and immediately obvious tactics.
Like yinsh, the play seems chaotic at first, but after a few
plays strategic patterns have begun to appear in my mind.

Tumblingdown has an interesting history. It was invented as
an entry to a game design contest sponsored at about.com. It
won the competition, but seems to have been immediately forgotten.
It came to my attention when someone pointed out the similarity
of its move style to that of Exxit.
