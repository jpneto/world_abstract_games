> > Good - Hex, Checkers

Each move

in Hex --> only one board position changes (one stone put on board)

in Checkers --> at least two positions are changed, but when you
capture two stones already four position are changed. As combinations
involving sacrifizing and capturing stones are crucial Checkers
definately hasn't much clarity, at least for a beginner.

in Othello --> at least two positions are changed, but theoretically up
to 19 positions can change.

in LOA --> only two positions change (rather high clarity!)
