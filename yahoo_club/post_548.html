I would be interested in seeing a sample game, just to be able to
visualize the dynamics. Hopefully the game would not necessitate the
constant use of a ruler to enforce its restrictions. Probably just
the use of a stone.

What would be the maximum distance that a player could play a stone?
Would there be a maximum length to the overall game?

Obvious, if the players were using a table that could be the
restrictive surface. On the floor, the game could get interesting.

And giving the curvature of the Earth and the general (mis)behaviour
of the Space-Time continuum, there is the potential of the game
looping back in on itself and creating some new possibilities.
