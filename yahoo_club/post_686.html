History, rules and list of pc games to the board game Pachisi & Ludo.

http://home.no.net/vkp3/index.html

The site lists 12 online, 36 freeware / abandonware and 25 commercial
games.

Pachisi =3D 'Twenty-five'. Descendants include Ludo (England) and
Parcheesi (USA). Ludo is also known as Mensch =C4rgere dich nicht
(Germany), Mens-erger-je-niet (the Netherlands), Parch=EDs or Parkase
(Spain), Le Jeu de Dada or Petits Chevaux (France), Non t'arrabbiare
(Italy), Fia med knuff (Sweden), Parqu=E9s (Colombia).
