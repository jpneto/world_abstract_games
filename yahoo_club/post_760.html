The WAG website is a collection of abstract games that can be played
with material restrictions, namely, one type of piece (games of
soldiers), two types of pieces (games of kings and soldiers) and
stackable pieces (games of towers).

As I mentioned in a previous post here, I'm just concerned with the
game specification, not the look, history, colors of the original
gameset. Sometimes, I read about a game that does use more material,
but can be translated to fit the previous criteria. Foxy is one of
those, but there are others. Of course, errors may appear in the
translation process, and for those, I'm sorry.
