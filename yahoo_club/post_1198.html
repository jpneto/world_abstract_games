Just as a suggestion, I would have White place any White piece he
likes onto any square in rank 1, and the corresponding Black piece
onto the corresponding square of rank 8, subject only to the condition
that at least one legal FRC opening setup can be completed from the
position. Then Black does the same thing, and so on until they've
completed the array. Then they play the game.
