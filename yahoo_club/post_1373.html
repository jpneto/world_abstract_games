Your description is technically more accurate, and programmers are
advised to take that approach. When one player has claimed more than
half of the cells, the game is effectively over and the program should
end the game and declare a winner. My way of stating the object is
simpler though and so I use that, assuming that programmers will know
what to do and that losing players will have the good sense to resign
if allowed to play on when they can't possibly win.
