Forchess (Four-Handed Chess Variant)

Author: wechselstrom

Date: 2001-06-28 19:53:05

Post ID: 98

For anyone interested in chess strategy, Forchess
(a four-handed chess variant played on a regular
chess board with an extra set of pieces) adds a new
dimension to gameplay by doubling the number of players. It
is typically played in teams, where the strategy is
similar to chess, with the exception that each player
must defend his army on two fronts. There is also a
variant in which all four players compete against each
other. In this form, the strategy becomes political
(imagine a hybrid cross between chess and Risk). To read
about Forchess, visit the official homepage:
http://www.intuitor.com/forchess
