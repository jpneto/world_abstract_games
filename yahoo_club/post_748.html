This is incorrect. During the time between the Essen fair and the
Nuremburg fair, I had custody of (but did not actually own) a very
nice copy of Pacru (the first copy to travel to the Americas),
courtesy of the game's author. After several games, I wrote a few very
brief comments about Pacru on the BoardGameGeek site before passing
the game along to another reviewer here in the USA, who will in turn
pass it along to another reviewer, etc. But I didn't write an article
on Pacru for AGM, or at least I haven't yet.

However, I did interview James Ernest for the magazine, and I have
contacted several other designers and/or publishers for potential
future interviews. I also submitted short reviews of James Kyle's
piecepack and Reiner Knizia's "Dice Games, Properly Explained" for
AGM. Unfortunately, all of those pieces are now fairly outdated.
