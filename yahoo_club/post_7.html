I've read some places where "depth" is defined as the
number of distinct levels at which human beings can
play. You can make them distinct by requiring, say,
that players at level (n+1) can consistently beat
players at level n in two games out of three. Then the
number of levels suggests how much human beings can
learn about the game, which also suggests how long it
will hold people's interest.
