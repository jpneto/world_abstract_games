I am new to this group and hope I am not overstepping bounds here. I
have joined because I love abstract strategy games and am interested
to see what the current 'buzz' is. I also maintain a hobby site (100%
free, no advertising) for the playing of such games completely
asynchronously (which is great when you don't have large blocks of
contiguous time). It's called Super Duper Games and can be found at
http://games.daltons.ca. I just finished implementing the commercial
game Cannon (with permission of course) and we have 12 other freely
available games as well (and more on the way). Our little community
is small, but growing, and very friendly. Feel free to come check us out!

I look forward to the discussions here.
