What do you do to prevent an illegal configuration from happening? It
is possible that someone put down a bishop, multiple pieces, and then
have the only space left is the same color square as the other bishop.
This is not legal. Basics that need to be followed:
1. There king must be inside both rooks to allow castling
2. The bishops must be on alternate colors

This is why simple placement isn't allowed. Like let's say we have this:
1. Bishop on white square
2. Rook on black square
3. Queen on black square
4. Knight on black square
5. King on black square

This is an illegal position. Similar could happen with rooks.
Usually when they do it via Random, they will put down bishops, then
rooks, and then the remaining pieces.
