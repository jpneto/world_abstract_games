International Wroclaw Go Championship 2002 (Poland)
Saturday November 9, 2002
8:00 am - 8:00 pm
(This event does not repeat.)
(

The next reminder for this event will be sent in 5 days, 3 minutes.)
Event Location: Institute of Theoretical Physics of the University of Wroclaw (Breslau), Poland

Notes:
Date:
9th, 10th November 2002


Place:

We have prepared two big playing rooms for 120 players (2 x 30 playing tables) as well as two smaller, more comfortable ones for 24 players of the top group (2 x 6 playing tables).


The Tournament System:

We play according to the Japanese rules:

There are 5 MacMahon rounds
Each player has 60 minutes of base time + 30 seconds of byo-yomi
All games are even, komi 5.5
The final results are decided by: MMS, SOS, SOSOS
Beginner players and players with ranking below 20 kyu play as 20 kyu
Tournaments Chief Referee: Roman A. Pszonka
