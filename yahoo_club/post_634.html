Iqishiqi is an interesting game. Potentially short, and definitely
difficult to extrapolate positions. A nice use of neutral pieces,
with that solitary stone acting as both the goal and referee.

The rules are not really that difficult to comprehend. There seems
to be a good number of tactics, such as placing stones to block your
opponent's path.

There are no draw conditions.
