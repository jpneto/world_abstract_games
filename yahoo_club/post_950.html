Perhaps an addition. I talked to the guy who invented Strat-o-Sphere
while promoting another of his inventions at the Fair in Essen three
years ago and I said to him that I prefer the original game. Guess what
he said??? He said, yes, I do so to, but a larger board wouldn't sell
that well, so Gigamic decided to release only a small version. The
first time he came to Essen he had a huge board with really big balls
(I think Billiard balls) -- truly an eye-catcher, and Gigamic bought
the rights. Many game designers are so eager to find a publisher that
they compromize their own games. Kris Burm didn't want to do that, so
he was forced to start his own publishing company.
