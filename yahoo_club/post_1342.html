Hmm... that actually sounds like a possibility.

What I did with Pethera-Tethera was have it so that once a player used
up their 10 disks, they had to pull one from the bottom and reenter it
on top, causing disks they drew to slide down. It is played on a
board like a Byzantine chessboard and had other restrictions also. It
is far from trivial, and is actually one of my better designs for
Stonehenge.
