I've been considering a one-dimensional version of Pente, played on a
circle with 19 points. (So I'm considering the circle as being one-
dimensional, since only its perimeter is the playing field.)

The object is to form a "pente," defined as a "line" of 5 stones in
which consecutive stones all have either 0, 1, 2, or 3 points between
them. Hence the last kind of win would have the pente almost
circumnavigating the circle.

Enemy stones can also be captured by forming a line of 4 stones,
consisting of two enemy stones sandwiched between two friendly
stones, and in which consecutive stones among the 4 have either 0, 1,
2, or 3 points between them. (Then there would be Linear Keryo-
Pente, in which a line of 3 could be captured the same way.)

Anyone have comments, or want to try a game by email (or maybe using
Zillions)?
