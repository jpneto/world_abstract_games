Author: ddyer0

Date: 2006-05-21 08:12:33

Post ID: 967

Boardspace.net has just added it's 8'th game, Gobblet.

Gobblet is a deceptively simple game, played on a 4x4 board.
The goal of the game is to make four in a row, but the pieces
are "gobblets" that nest like Russian dolls.

There's a kick-ass robot player to show you the ropes.
