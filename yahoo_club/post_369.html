By the way, I'm going to "host" a Quoridor tournament on Richard's
play-by-email server starting early in January. This is a free and
easy-to-use server, so even if you're not yet a user I hope you'll
sign on and join the tournament if you're a Quoridor fan.

Play is by e-mail through the server, with 30 days to complete all
your moves. So far there are about 15 players, and the limit is 20.
It will be organized into two (maybe 3) rounds of round-robin play,
about 4 or 5 players per round. If there are lots of ties in round 1
then there might be two rounds in round 2, hence a third round of the
winners for a play-off. The final champion wins a new, shrink-wrapped
Quoridor set.

To join the tournament, sign up at Richard's and message me. My user
id there is markthomps.
