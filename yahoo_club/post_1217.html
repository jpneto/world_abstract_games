Pool Checkers joins the 2008 IAGO World Tour

Author: Rich Hutnik

Date: 2007-11-10 23:59:01

Post ID: 1217

Pool Checkers will be part of the 2008 IAGO World Tour. It will be in
Chicago on July 13-19, 2008. The Tournament name is:
"The American Pool Checkers Association's 42nd Annual National Tournament"

Check out either of these two pages for more information:
http://www.hspccc.com/apcachi08.htm

or here:
http://home.comcast.net/~homansquare/wsb/html/view.cgi-home.html-.html

For those who don't know what Pool Checkers is, it is a version of
checkers that had origins in Europe, but gained popularity in America.
The checkers can jump backwards and the king pieces have greater
mobility than standard checker kings.
