Almost forgot the last rule about forming 'houses'.

3. If the player forms a 'house', a triangle and square in the same
move, they may move a single friendly stone and two opposing stones.

In the extra movement of two opposing stones, it was also allowed to
either move two seperate stones or one stone two steps.

And the extra movement of a friendly stone, it was allowed to move
the same one again as long as it did not return to its previous
position. Such would violate the restriction against repetition of
position.
