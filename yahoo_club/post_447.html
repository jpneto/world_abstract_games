Excuse me while a do a little self-promotion.

Try Rigellan Sii-od. There's a Zillion implementation at their
website. It's a three-player chess-type game played on a hexagonal
field. The object of the game is not to lose, so right off you're
considered playing two hostile opponents

The movement of each piece is strange but simple, and there are
promotion pieces which liven the game without given a player too much
of an advantage.

Someone e-mailed and asked me how I came up with the idea for this
game. Jokingly, I replied, "I forgot to wear my aluminium-foil hat
one day." I was surprised not to hear from them again.
