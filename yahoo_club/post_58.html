Before getting to my point a definition
first:

Capture Sequence: A series of consecutive turns in which
each turn includes a capture.

I have thought
more on this topic and think I have a way to eliminate
any possibility of a draw through infinite capture
sequences by stipulating the following:

Anbody who
repeats a turn in a capture sequence must announce the
repetition after the turn. After which the opponent cannot
respond the same way as was done before to any of the
previous incarnation of this turn in this capture
sequence. So within a capture sequence, the responses to
every repetition of a turn must be different.


So the full rule on irreversible gain would include
the above rule and the one below:

The person
who makes the last capture in the capture sequence
get one stone toward the fullfillment of their
contracts.
