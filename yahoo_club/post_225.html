A friend of mine told me today about an abstract game he saw at a
game store but he can't recall the name. His description was as
follows.

The game board was about a foot square and checked, light/dark like a
chess board, and 4X4 or it may have been 5X5.

The pieces looked like inverted cups and when he picked one up there
was a smaller one under it and another even smaller one under that.

The board and the pieces were wood or at least were made to look like
wood. Some pieces were light and some dark.

The game was on display near the cash register and when he commented
on it the cashier mentioned that it had won an award.


If anyone knows what game this describes I would appreciate hearing.
I'm intrigued by the idea of nested pieces. It seems to me that such
an arrangement opens some interesting possibilities for movement
mechanics in a game.
