Competition is what gets you media coverage, and media coverage is
what gets new members. This is why competition is important. It
isn't the end all and be all. The organization is meant to also
provide resources for people to play, promote modern abstracts, and
also get abstracts as a whole in the public eye.

Anyhow, the petition is merely one of multiple tools to get people to
express themselves. It isn't the answer byt any means.

Also, can we agree here that people play abstract strategy games, like
any sport, to win? It is a battle of the mind. Maybe you don't play
for big stakes, but you do play for the challenge of besting someone.
If it were simply just playing to beat problems, there are puzzles
for that.
