Boardspace.net has been running for 2 years now. We're currently
hosting seven games, most you won't find any where else.

the Gipf games: Zertz, Yinsh, and Punct. Also featuring the only
robot opponnets for these games that you will find anywhere.

Lines of Action, the classic connection game by Claude Soucie

Plateau, an unique stacking and capturing game, with some hidden
information.

Trax, a very deep tile laying game which uses only ONE kind of tile.

and Hex, one of the simplest and most elegant games ever.
