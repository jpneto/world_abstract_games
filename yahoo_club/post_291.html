> Do you have the rules for Sahara? Can you give a summary of them?

Not easy. I played it about half a year ago:

At the beginning the pieces are in pairs at the corners of the board,
on the left side "white" on the right side is "black".

Photo:
http://www.toysinternational.cc/images/Sahara.jpg

The pieces are pyramides which are moving by rotating to an adjacent
triangular "square" of either color. Pieces cannot rotate over
another piece. A piece is captured when all sides are bordered by the
edge of the board and/or another piece of either color. The first
player to capture a piece wins the game (as in "The Game of Susan").
