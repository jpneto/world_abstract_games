Dice can be right-handed or left-handed, according to the arrangement
of the pips on the face. Although the opposite faces equal seven,
looking around the 1&6 axis the other four faces can be clockwise or
counter-clockwise. Basicly, for example, the 3 and 4 are switched.

There are also variations on the angle of the faces, but this is of
no consequence to this particular game.
