New MSG game: Mosaic

http://www.marksteeregames.com/Mosaic_rules.pdf

Mosaic is a game of territory and aggression. Reversi tiles are played on a hex hex board. Multiple flip sequences sometimes occur in one turn. Interestingly, a number of flips may occur after the board has completely filled with tiles, though of course Mosaic is a naturally finite game.

I've been playing Mosaic for several days and it's shaping up to be a very interesting game.

Mosaic can be played at gwc:
