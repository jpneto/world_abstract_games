Hello. Here's an idea or rather a theme that maybe of some
interesting:

Imagine a game where the players accumulate points in some
way, and at the end the player with the higher number of points,
receive the points accumulated by the oponent. The two player than
switch sides and play again. the player that receive the greater
number of points win.

In this game it is obviously not good to win by too much for it
reduce one's own score. And worse if you lose. So when has to strive
for an almost tie where one is on top.

This can obviously applied to Go. what other games can be made?
