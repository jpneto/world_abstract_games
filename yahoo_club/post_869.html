I thought of a possible Pente variant the other day, which I think
would probably be playable and would probably reduce the first player
advantage. My working title is "Knight Pente". (Or maybe "Ritter Renju"?)

The idea is simple. The first player places a stone in the center of
the board. After that, every stone must be played a knight's move from
some stone (friendly or not) already on the board.

I would probably allow captures of either 2 or 3 or maybe even 4
stones in a straight line. Even getting 4 in a row could be somewhat
challenging, though, so that might be the object in a shorter variant.

This needs testing, but the idea attracts me. Maybe others would also
like to try it out.
