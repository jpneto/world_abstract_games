The &quot;Gene Lindsay&quot; 3-move U.S. National (ACF) Joins IAGO World Tour

Author: Rich Hutnik

Date: 2008-04-22 03:39:36

Post ID: 1430

The "Gene Lindsay" 3-move U.S. National is the main event in the
United States for professional checkers (American Checker Federation).
It is open to all people. This events joins the IAGO World Tour. It
will be July 21-25, 2008 in Las Vegas. For more information on the
event, visit:
http://www.usacheckers.com/tournamentannouncements.php#184

For more information on the IAGO World Tour, visit:
