My comment to:

http://www.di.fc.ul.pt/~jpn/gv/kuba.htm

> KO rule - If a player push a set of stones, with an opponent stone
> at the end, the next player cannot slide back on that row/column.
> He must wait for the turn after.

This is not the official rule.

For instance:

--> 0XX at the edge of the board

White pushes one black stone off the board.
Black may push back, because the position isn't repeated.

For instance:

--> 0X.X

White pushes on black stone to the right.
Black pushes one black stone and one white stone to the left.

The exact rule is as follows:

If a player pushes stones in one direction, the other player may not
push these stones back in the opposite direction in his next move if
the full board position is repeated, but must wait one turn.

Still, this rule isn't perfect as there are full board repetitions
after 4 moves.

It would be best if the rule would simply be:

It's not permitted to repeat a full board position during a game.
