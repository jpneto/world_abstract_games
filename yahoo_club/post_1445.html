Asbury Park Chess Festival 2008 will attempt to break world record

Author: Rich Hutnik

Date: 2008-07-17 17:06:57

Post ID: 1445

You can see the site here:
http://asburyparkchess.org/

The event is for charity. It will be in New Jersey (Asbury Park) on
September 27, 2008. It is going for the record for most simultaneous
games of chess going on at same time in same event.

If you are in the area, stop by and see if you can help out.
