TwixT was really invented in 1957(/58?), the date is from Erwin
Glonnegger, one of the most well-known German game experts and former
head of Ravensburger. In 1998, there was a limited new edition of
TwixT in Germany to celebrate its 40th anniversary including copies
of the first recorded TwixT games played in the coffeehouse Hawelka
(still one of Vienna's famous meeting points for artists) between A.
Randolph and Herbert Feuerstein. I believe that Kosmos Games knew the
correct date for the anniversary.
