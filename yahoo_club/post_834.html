A List of Abstract Strategy Stacking Games

Author: Ralf

Date: 2005-11-16 08:43:20

Post ID: 834

This is a list of 'true' stacking games based on abstract strategy. They also have perfect information. If you know more games, please let me know:
Bashni (---, pre-1875)Lasca (Emanuel Lasker, 1911)Pasta (Alvin Paster, 1956)Focus (Sid Sackon, 1963)Stapeldammen (?, late 1960s)Netak (Ralf Gering, 1973)Tricolor (Valentin Siena, 1973)Quantum (Philip Slater, 1975)Tower of Power (Michael Baldwin, 1976)Emergo (Christian Freeling, early 1980s)Ombagi (Reinhold Wittig, 1981)Colonnes de Trois (?, 1986)Chain Reaction (Sid Sackson, 1993)Pogo (Laurent Escoffier, 1993)Avalam Bitaka (Philippe Deweys, 1995)Accasta (Dieter Stein, 1998)Hong Kong (Reiner Knizia, 1998)Tumbling Down (Michael Shuck, 2000)Dvonn (Kris Burm, 2001)Abstract Puluc (Joao Pedro, 2002)Ice Towers (Abdrew Looney, 2002)Hex-It (Vincent Everaert, 2003)Accasta Pari (Dieter Stein, 2004)Abande (Dieter Stein, 2005)Byte (Mark Steere, 2005)Rondell (Ralf Gering, 2005)
Clones were excluded: Crossfire, Gomony, Stack-Up Checkers, Stax, etc.

Games with no perfect information were excluded: Plateau, Stack, etc.

Ralf
