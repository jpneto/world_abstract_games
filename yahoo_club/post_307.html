http://www.di.fc.ul.pt/~jpn/gv/dispatch.htm

My review:

Dispatch isn't a new game, but a variant of Pentamino Go which was
described by Wayne Schmittberger in his book "New Rules for Classic
Games" (in: "Omino Go").

Schmittberger's original game is more elegant, since it goes without
artificial and unnecessary rules such as "dispatching" while they
result in a game which is pretty much the same as Dispatch, except
that it has more combinatorial variety.
