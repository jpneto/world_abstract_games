Abstract games have been given the short end of the stick in the table
game arena. I believe this should be dealt with. As a result, I am
looking to get an association started to promote abstract strategy
games. There is a petition you can "sign" on the Web to express your
support, and a forum to discuss the idea.

The forum is here on the Super Duper Games Site:
http://forums.superdupergames.org/viewforum.php?f=3D14

If you are interested in having one started, please sign the
petition:
http://www.petitiononline.com/asggroup/petition.html

The name IAGO (International Abstract Games Organization) was proposed
as a possible name by Bill Taylor on rec.games.abstract, and I happen
to think that is a cool name myself.

Anyhow, if you want to get involved, pleas sign the petition and visit
the forum.

Thank you for your time...
