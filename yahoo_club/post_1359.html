Fanorona is now up for real-time play at Boardspace.net

Fanorona is a classic (but largely forgotten) capturing game. It's a
mystery to me why it isn't better know, because it's a load of fun to
play.

The game starts with the board full except for one space. The first
few moves are mass captures, which leaves a few pieces of wreckage to
be cleaned up in a subtle positional game.
