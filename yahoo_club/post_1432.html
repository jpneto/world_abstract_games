There's a new game at Boardspace, called Traboulet. Traboulet is a
marble pushing game, mechanically similar to Abalone, but a completely
different game. The physical game is one of those mechanical marvels
that's fun just to use. The robots are ready to "introduce" you to
the game.
http://boardspace.net/english/about_kuba.html

There will be a Hive (with Mosquito) tournament starting June 1. As
with last year's tournament, John Yianni has promised to award a
trophy to the winner, and also, mosquitos to the top 16 players in the
tournament. This is already sure to be the biggest tournament ever
at boardspace, but I will cap the participation at 128 players.
http://boardspace.net/cgi-bin/tournament-signup.cgi?tournamentid=3D13

Finally, there will be a Zertz tournament targeted to beginners, also
starting June 1 or when the first 16 players have signed up. The
established master players will not be playing, so the competition
should be friendly and not overwhelming for newcomers.
http://boardspace.net/cgi-bin/tournament-signup.cgi?tournamentid=3D12
