Personally, I'm fine with all of them. I will comment though that I would
expect the last one to be "Settlers of Catan". However, if the designer
intended it to be written that way that's fine with me and it might cause
me to to ask some questions. Those questions, whether answered or not,
might even add to the mythology of the game. For example, I might wonder
if the emphasis of the game is based more on its location than its
occupants. On the other hand, I might just write it off as a gimmick.

-- Greg

P.S. Revisiting my example, "TwixtT", I think that particular rendering
emphasizes the "connection between towers" aspect of the game. Some might
call it a gimmick, but for me, game appeal can be enhanced to a certain
degree by the judicious use of gimmickry.
