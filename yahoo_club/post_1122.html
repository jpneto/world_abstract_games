Looking over the current list of signatures, I couldn't help but
notice that all 27 signers appear to be men. Perhaps something can be
done to redress this gross imbalance. It may be that IAGO's strong
emphasis on competition (crowining a world champion for individual
games and crowning an overall world champion) has alienated some women
who might otherwise be interested in joining. Maybe if the
competitive aspect of the association were de-emphasized, and the
social aspect (e.g. exchanging gaming ideas and posting thoughts on
strategy and tactics) were emphasized, IAGO might achieve a better
balance?
