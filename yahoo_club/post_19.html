Hi again

Me and Cameron Brown are playing
and studying Gonnect,
a very nice crossover
between Go and Hex.

We are collecting a lot of
notes, and possibly there
will be an article in
Abstract Games Magazine n.6
about the
game.

There are some possibilities that Gonnect may
have
superior depth than Go, because it seems it
creates
more complex endgames, which is very interesting, if

you add to the fact that the winning goal is a lot

more simpler (connect two opposite
edges).

There is already a gonnect server at Richard's pbm
site
and I invite anyone who wants to try it.

The
www is
http://www.gamerz.net/pbmserv/gonnect.html
and my userid is jpn.

The syntax is: Gonnect
challenge -medium id1 id2
for 13x13 games (the standard
board size)
