Here's what you don't do: You don't show up at Super Duper Games,
declare all of the games there to be "boring," and then try to
establish your intellectual superiority with bluster, belligerence,
and hysterical temper tantrums. That's actually a formula to get
yourself projectile ejected with a nice, rosy boot print on your ass.
(The fact that Aaron tolerated Ralf's shenanigans as long as he did is
testimony to Aaron's generosity and sense of fair play.)

Here's how you establish intellectual credibility in an abstract
gaming venue: You learn the games that are played there, you play them
hard, play them well, and you excel in the ratings. Does any of this
make sense?
