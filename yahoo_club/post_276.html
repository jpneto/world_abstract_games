A game of Brussels Sprouts on n points always ends in the same number
of moves. Therefore for any given n, either the first or second
player will always win. The "winning strategy" is to make any move
whatsoever and await the inevitable.

Brussels Sprouts is a joke game, a mathematical curiosity.
