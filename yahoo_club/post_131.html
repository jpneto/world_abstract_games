Here is a related game, played by N players: the
US$100 dollar game (described by Geoff Wong):

"1.
Each player starts the game with $0, the bank starts
with $100.

2. After a period of negotiation
each player writes down a number (secretly) between 1
and 1000.

3. The winner is the player with
the highest unique number (N).

4. The payout
is $ (100 / the winning player's number).

:
As you can see winning with a large number (1000)
will only gain you 10 cents; ideally you wish to win
with the number 1; but this requires a certain amount
of negotiation. I only know of one game where the
bank has had to pay out the full $100, and very few
where the bank has paid out more than
$5."
