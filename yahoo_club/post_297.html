Pagoda is manufactured by a company called Pair-of-Dice Games. They briefly
describe the game on their website and have pictures. They also show a link
to a review of the game but the link doesn't work for me. I've dealt with
these people before (bought a game from them) and they seem very nice. If
you e-mail them they may give you an updated link or just attach a copy of
the review.

Steppe is manufactured by DMR Games and I think I have a paper copy of the
rules at home. If you e-mail me ( mschoessow@...
) a fax number I'll try to find the
rules and fax them to you tomorrow.

I have no information on Domain other than knowing it is a Parker Bros. game
from 1983 and seeing a picture of it at abstractstrategy.com.

You probably already know this, but Abstract Games magazine has promised
articles on all three games in their next issue.
