Greetings,

I read both messages, I appreciate your compliments. I view it as a
4x4x4x4 matrix with the first three dimensions as they are and the
4th as a periodic convention (no defined endpoints). I see the
fourth dimension as cyclical, with incremental movement along
adjacent quadrants.

I would love to read your 6D Chess rules. I see no reason why the
Time Vectors game board cannot support more games as in GRYB. The
game board is a basic structure. I welcome any efforts to brainstorm
games using it. I hope I get a chance to with play them all.

Kudos back to you and to all abstract gamers out there.

Best regards,
David Barnhart [barneypaloma]
