I created a GeekList "Stacking Games" at boardgamegeek.com awhile ago:

http://www.boardgamegeek.com/geeklist.php3?action=view&listid=2246

Perhaps it's useful to see what others generally understand by the term
"stacking game" -- one contributor added Jenga :-)

My understanding is that a "stack" in a stacking game can't be just the
physical structure without any function related to the game and its
rules. Also, looking at single pieces and there attributes, a stack must
have its own attribute set, e.g. maximum height, prominent top position etc.
