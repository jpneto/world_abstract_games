Some years ago, me, Bill Taylor and Cameron Browne discussed about this same subject and we came up with several 3-player game extensions that tried to minimize the Petty Diplomacy problem. One of those mechanisms was to micro-compensate the player victim of a two-way alliance in order to make these alliances unstable ones.
One of those games is Triad (http://www.cameronius.com/games/triad/)
by Cam.

Those conversations were included in a chapter on the book "Mathematical Games, Abstract Games".
