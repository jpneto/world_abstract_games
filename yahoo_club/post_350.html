> Can you tell me what Sachovany means?
> Something with Chess, but what exactly?

Sachovani, the word actually used on the pages,
is "the act of playing chess", said informally.

A bit like saying "chessing" or "die Schachierung",
although saying such a thing in Czech is a bit more natural.

> (Chessling?)

An excellent translation for chessling yes, I would
say, considering chessling doesn't exist in English
and sounds like an informal nice way to say playing
chess around.

We only lose a pun with the author's name, Ling.

There is a nice echo of chovani too, which is the
act of breeding (animals), bringing up (a child),
nursing (an idea).
