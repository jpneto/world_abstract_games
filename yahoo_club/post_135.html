Hi Miss V,

One thing you could check is
http://www.gamingdumpster.com/wgd/ . That's a "world gamers database" where people
can go and enter themselves by location and gaming
interests, with an e-mail link. Then other people can find
them and make contact. It sounds like a good idea but
I don't know how well it works. I just took a look
and found there is one person listed in Winchester,
VA.

Then there are clubs connected with particular games,
like the U.S. Chess Federation
(http://www.uschess.org/) or the American Contract Bridge League
(http://www.acbl.org/), and many others. Some of them have listings of
local clubs on their websites.

Good luck with
everything! Maybe other people will also have
suggestions.
