Excellent! And very simple. The
sort of rule whose abstract
consequences I love to

About your bad
feeling that players may overuse the moving

possibility, what if you put it only as an alternative choice

between moving and putting a new piece (if it's not
already what
you mean)? That way players wouldn't
often move in the opening.
Because they rather need
to increase their presence on the board
by
putting new pieces.

Or you don't start the board
empty - but with the othello/reversi
starting
position for example - and players don't choose between

moving or putting a new piece... Instead, you always
move, but a
new piece also always appears right on
the square you left.

I'm ready to
play!

more
influenced by the board size and a little
less positional than

example.
