piecepack game design competition winner announced

Author: Michael Schoessow

Date: 2003-03-05 18:20:29

Post ID: 406

On March 3rd the winners of the 3rd Piecepack Game Design Competition was
announced. First place went to Rob LeGood for his game New City, and second
place went to Tim Schutz for IceFloe.

Although many of the games are themed, there are some that are either
abstract or which have an abstract feel. One of the most interesting
abstracts is a unique tile-placing game called Decay.

More details on the contest results as well as rule sets for all the games
entered can be found at www.piecepack.org.

-Mike Schoessow
