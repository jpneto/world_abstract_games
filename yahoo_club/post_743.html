According to Stephen Tavener and D. U. Thibault, Foxy (a pseudo-
stacking game designed by Alex Randolph)is played with 11 stones each
color, not with 8 neutral stones (grey) and 12 partial stones (blue,
yellow) as stated by Joao Neto in his "World of Abstract Games".
