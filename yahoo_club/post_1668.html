???? Christian, if Checkers isn't a stacking game, I don't know what the fuck is. A stack fuctions uniquely and independently as a single unit in Checkers. It's not like the second story is used for spare checker storage. If you're going to convince this native English speaker that Checkers is not a stacking game, whoa.. lol You've sure got your work cut out for you, Christian.

Yes, stacks in Checkers are limited to a height of two, and must be comprised of like colored checkers. And a stack can be represented instead by flipping over a singleton and showing the crown side. So what?? Every stack in every stacking game can be represented somehow with out using actual stacks.

Checkers is the original stacking game. If you're proposing some definition for stacking that requires stacks to be taller than two, or have mixed colors or whatever, count me out.
