I'm not a master at any game, but if it's a help I would say that one
characteristic of a beautiful move is that it works on several
different levels.

I don't think a move in tic-tac-toe could have this characteristic
(although perhaps it may be beautiful in other respects), because the
game doesn't seem to have enough depth to elicit a move that works on
several different levels.
