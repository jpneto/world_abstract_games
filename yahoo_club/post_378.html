Mark's Diagonal Twixt is included as a variant in my Zillions Twixt
package. I call it "Diagonal 45 Twixt" because the border rows are at
a 45 degree angle to the grid. I also include "Diagonal 63 Twixt"
where the borders run parallel to the direction of the links,
approximately 63 degrees (or 27 degrees, depending on how you measure
it) relative to the grid of holes. Some screen shots can be seen at
http://www.geocities.com/twixtplayer/ZTwixt2.html

The Twixt package is free, but you have to buy Zillions to use it,
and the price just went up. We could send a diagonal Twixt image back
and forth via email, or a Y board, if you want. I won't have time to
