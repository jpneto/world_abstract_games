There's a new Yahoo group that may be of interest
to you: The Virtual games
club, at
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/virtualgameclub
There's a link at that site to download the core
software.
Then download scripts for whatever games you'd like to
play.
If you already know the rules, you can play games in
real-time!
There are not many abstract games there now, but they
are fairly easy
to add. I have posted Twixt there,
and I'm looking for
opponents. Handicaps are
available. I also include a couple
of Twixt
variants.
Zertz is there, and Billabong. More on the way.
