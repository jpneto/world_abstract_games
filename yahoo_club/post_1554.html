Now about the game itself. I haven't actually played, so this
is purely speculative, but won't the "go for zero" strategy
completely dominate? It's trivial to just keep dropping stones
anywhere they don't connect, so unless the other players adopt
the "zero" strategy too, zero will always win. If all players
adopt the zero strategy, then the board will fill up with unconnected
stones, until a win is determined at the last moment when only
the corners remain unclaimed.
