For those of you who enjoy Cannon (or are interested in knowing more
about it) I have setup the Cannon Database. This is the official
archive of Cannon games (not just games played at Super Duper Games).
It features a database with search capabilities, a free-form area to
play through these games or setup your own positions, and a (mildly
intelligent) AI opponent. It can be found at
http://cannon.daltons.ca. If you've never played the game, you really
must try it. It has quickly become a favourite for me. Of course,
you can play for free against real people at Super Duper Games
(http://games.daltons.ca).
