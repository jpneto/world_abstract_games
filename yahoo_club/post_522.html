The newest trend I observed at one of the world largest game fairs which was
just a few weeks ago in Essen, Germany (150,000 visitors) is that, more and
more, inventors publish/market their games on their own. It's important that
you retain the copyright of your games so that you are in control of your
masterpieces. Another possibility would be to offer the game at Richard's
PBeM server or at brettspielwelt.de. Brettspiel.welt de offers so far only a
few abstract games: Yinsh, Dvonn and Cups (called "Bohnduell").
