The reason for the rules are as follows.

I
want this game be mostly positional. That is the
placement of the pieces have great effect on the game. Thus
the rules about movement.

Also because of
above, I also wanted the game enphasis on placement, so
I decided to restrict movement to absolutely
necessary, so the rule that movement allowed when pieces are
threatened.

There is however downsides to these rules which is why
I try to balance it. For example. isolated pieces
becomes very vulnerable, so I made them invulnerable.
This counterbalance however might make the game to
'rigid', ie. not enough movement allowed, so I should have
also ammended that isolated pieces can be 'threatened'
(though not captured)

The reason for the
restriction on placement is to have the game focused in the
beginning and keep the opening manageable and more or less
independent of board size.

that I need more sample games, however at this time I
haven't entirely tested everything out. And needs
playtesters to help in generating sample games. Hopefully
some of the people here can help.
