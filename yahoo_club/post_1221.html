Boardspace.net will host a tournament for the true abstract game
generalist; each game in each match will be different, chosen among
the 15 games currently available.

http://boardspace.net/cgi-bin/tournament-signup.cgi?tournamentid=3D10

Play will start approximately December first, and continue at a fairly
leisurely pace until only one player remains undefeated. Timeouts to
learn the games and practice are encouraged!
