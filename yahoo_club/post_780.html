Hex can now be played at http://www.boardspace.net/

I was surprised to find that Hex is a really interesting game,
not just one with nice theoretical properties. Most Hex games
play very fast, because after 10 moves or so it's obvious that
one player or the other has won. Curiously, it seems to always be
the more experienced player, no matter which color he plays.
