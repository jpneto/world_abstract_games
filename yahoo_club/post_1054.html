To all friends of Abstract Games Community:
FYI: Buenos Aires, Argentina. December 17th, will be the day of Abstract
Games: CODASPORTS (NGO for develop new games and sports) will be
organized the 4th edition of Mind Games (in spanish, JUEGOS DE LA
MENTE). No entry fee, 8 games in the championships (ABALONE, AMAZONAS,
AWALE, FANORONA, HALMA, HEX, LINES OF ACTION & OTHELLO) with the
possibility of play in three games simultaneous.
Are you in Argentina, living or visit? send e-mail to CODASPORTS, or by
this way for play in the tournament. The official website (in spanish)
is www.juegosdelamente2006.com.ar

All results will be published in the website.
Thank you for your attention and stay in touch.

Kind Regards to the Abstract Gamers Community!

Ricardo Acuna - Argentina - www.codasports.com.ar
