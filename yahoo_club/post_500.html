Just from looking at the board and pieces on BoardgameGeek
http://www.boardgamegeek.com/viewitem.php3?gameid=2453 I see that
Universe can be played with a Blokus set. Universe uses four sets of
pentominoes, nothing smaller, on a board with is contained within a
16x16 grid. The extra Blokus pieces could be used to delinieate the
border of a Universe grid. So, if you're feeling nostalgic you could
play another game. Here is a link to Universe rules:
http://www.geocities.com/twixtplayer/Universe1.html and here is the
game on BoardGameGeek: http://www.boardgamegeek.com/viewitem.php3?
gameid=6547
