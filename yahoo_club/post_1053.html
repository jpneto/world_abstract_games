Hi malaschitz,

Thanks for your comments. Good suggestion! Limiting direction of
travel would indeed make the game more Plakoto-like and would also
address another problem that has emerged: players with pinned pieces
can move a piece back and forth to wind down the clock and force a
stalemate.

I was initially worried that this rule change would mean that long
sections of the track would never be used, but thinking about it
more I see that this should not be the case.

After playing the game a bit it turns out that another rule change
is needed: players should be able to change direction if they land
on a crossing mid-move. I'll update the rules shortly.

Regards,
Cameron
