I've uploaded the rules to BGG (with permission from Greg):
http://boardgamegeek.com/game/3660
They should show up in the Files section in the next day or so, I guess.

Anyway, yes, you can stack your paper onto your opponent's paper. A
stack is owned by the player on top, and can be moved as a stack (in a
straight line up to N spaces for height N) or can be pushed over (and
spread out over N spaces in a straight line).
