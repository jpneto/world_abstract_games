> ORBIT (standard version)

> 6. An "orbit" is a connected group which completely encircles one
or
> more points of the board. When an orbit is formed, any opposing
> stones within the orbit are removed from the board, and it is
> thereafter prohibited for the opponent to play within that orbit.

Can an orbit capture another orbit by encircling it?

TruthForum

Absolutely! By the same token, a half-orbit can "encircle" an orbit or another half-orbit, resulting in shared territory within the orbit or within the inner half-orbit. Also, an orbit can capture a section of a half-orbit, resulting in its dissolution.
