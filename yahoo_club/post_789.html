The world of abstract games website reached 250 games using one type
of stones for each player (and played on a square or hex board).

When this section started, back in 2000, my estimation of the total
games I would find would be around 100. They are 250 now and counting
(I still have 50 more to write due to my net searches). Together with
the games of Kings and games of Towers (this last section could
include the previous two) the total of posted games is around 450.

I suppose this is a never ending task, but the existence of more than
500 games with such strict material restrictions is, imho, amazing
(oc, some games are way better than others).
