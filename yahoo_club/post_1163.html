That is very good question. In principle I should say
that any game including a random component should be
discounted as a Abstract Str. Game. HOWEVER the whole
purpose of 960 is to bring out pure strategic (cq
tactical) thinking in a game in which hard study is
such an important part of the way it is played. On
that count, and because the randomness is only part of
the initial configuration, perhaps it should be
included.
