What I'm trying to do is, in some ways, mimic aspects of behaviors
exhibited in military encounters, or explore behaviors of tiny
one-celled imaginary creatures, entirely by using a few basic chess
pieces and associative rules. There's no attempt to reproduce reality;
it's more in the nature of Conway's game of Life, which is part of the
inspiration for these designs. How is Life categorized? As I was much
more mailman that mathematician in my career, I leave it to others to
categorize my stuff. I do admit to being curious about the categories,
though. [Sometimes I want to know just what the heck it is I'm doing...]
