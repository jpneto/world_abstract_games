I don't normally create variants, but upon reading portions of
Cameron Browne's "Connection Games" I was inspired to come up with
Box Hex and Net Y, both of which have rule sheets on my website.

Someone initially made the criticism that Box Hex is essentially a
two dimensional game, and I argued vehemently that it's three
dimensional. But now in retrospect the argument for two dimensions
seems to have some merit.

Neither of these two variants are played anywhere online that I know
of.
