> in LOA --> only two positions change (rather high clarity!)

I'd say that LOA does not have very good clarity at all. For every
move, you have to:

1) Count the number of pieces in the row/column/diagonal,
2) Make sure the destination point is that distance away,
3) Remember whether it was your pieces or your opponent's pieces
that you can jump over, then
4) Remember whether it was your pieces or your opponent's pieces
that you can jump over.

Admittedly 4) is obvious since you then capture the piece :) but my
point is that each move requires a signficant amount of mental
bookkeeping just to determine whether it's legal or not, which I
believe points to a lack of clarity.
