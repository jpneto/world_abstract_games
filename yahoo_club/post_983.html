Hello all,

I am pleased to announce the beginning of a new podcast: Abstract
Gamer.

In this podcast, I will discuss... wait for it... Games! With a
focus on abstract strategy games. (Bet you didn't see that coming,
did you?) OK, I'm sure you did. ;) I expect new episodes to be
published every couple weeks.

In the current episode (Episode 2) I introduce myself again, discuss
the Shogi family of games, and go into details about Chu Shogi. I'm
still finding my voice, but I have fixed some of the technical
issues from the previous episode. Thanks to all who gave me feedback
on that episode.

Episode 3 will be about handicaps, the GIPF project and GIPF in
detail. I'm in post production and expect to release it next week.

I hope you enjoy the show. Feel free to contact me at
abstractgamer@... with any comments, or leave a comment on the
blog (http://www.abstractgamer.com)
