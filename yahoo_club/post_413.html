A web-bot translation as follows:

"Laska" belongs to the group of the column or tower plays
(English "column games"). It is common to this family of
board plays that the opponent removed Spielsteine are jumped
over, taken not however by the board. In such a way
conquered and/or caught Spielsteine to columns and/or to
towers are stacked. The oldest of these column plays is
followed Baschni "(also called" Bashne") ", of "Laska".
1956 followed with "Pasta" the third column play, 1965 with
"focus" (also under the names "Domination" or "stack" well-known)
fourth, end of the 1960er years with
"Stapeldammen" the fifth. Center of the 1980er years were
added "Emergo" and "HexEmergo". For years the family of the
column plays became larger the 1990er around at least five
further representatives ("Baschni per ", "Gomony", "plateau", "Topper"
and its variants, "Tumbling down").
