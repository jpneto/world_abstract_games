Years ago, I came across a little book which transformed the way I
looked at the world and games. I cannot say this of many books.

It was both enjoyable and thought-provoking. And it still influences
me.

This book was "Finite and Infinite Games" by James Carse.

It is basically a philosopical dissertation about the nature of the
Universe. That it is an infinite game divided into finite ones. We
are all playing finite games, and while playing we participate in an
infinite game.

Within our finite games, we have established rules, rewards and
penalties. These can be both changing and non-changing. All decided
by mutual agreement or settle by aggressive competition.

There is much more. I recommend this particular book to everyone.
You will not be disappointed by its content.
