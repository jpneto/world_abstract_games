Greg, thank you for picking Christine as your example. I had the
pleasure of working with her on one series of games listed there. She

would hope to see her back sometime in the future.

Should anyone wish to find more women chess variant designers, you may
scroll through the contributors names here:
http://chessvariants.org/index/personq.php and click to each of their
designs posted at The Chess Variant Pages. I'm sure at most any game
site that lists designer's names, you would find women.
