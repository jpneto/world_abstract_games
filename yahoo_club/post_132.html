I recall reading somewhere (how's that for citing
my sources?) that Scientific American actually ran
this contest in the 1980s. A million dollars was at
stake with the actual payout to be divided by the
number of entries. The number of entries you could
submit was limited only to the biggest number you could
fit on an index card. Naturally people wrote in with
entries like "googleplex to the googleplex" which reduced
the pot to zero. Nobody won because the judges could
not figure out which number was the biggest.
