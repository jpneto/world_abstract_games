We prefer baseball statistics. Seriously, if historical accuracy is
important, calling a moving line in Epaminondas a phalanx is a
mistake. Indeed, crediting Epaminondas with inventing the phalanx is
inaccurate (full disclosure: I repeated this error in my Epaminondas

innovation was to use an oblique phalanx to pound one side of the
enemy's line.
