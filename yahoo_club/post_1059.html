http://www.mathpuzzle.com/ has some information, including a diagram,
of a figure called "Klein's Quartic Curve". It's a figure with 56
vertices, each one connected to 3 others, which has loads of
symmetries. There are 84 edges, I think. The smallest cycle is a
heptagon, and if you follow an edge and go left then right then left
then right and so on, you return to your starting place after eight
edges, forming a thing called a Petrie polygon.

I was considering what sort of games might be played on such a
surface. Perhaps Black and White could alternately claim edges, and
the first one who completes a cycle in his color wins.
