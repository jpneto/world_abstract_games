Hello, All.

Signup has begun for the Internet Abstract Games Decathlon. This is a little competition to be run on various internet game sites over the period of the next few months. The idea is to pick 10 abstract games that cover a wide variety of play mechanics and give people a chance to compete against each other in an overall competition, as opposed to their favorite game.

It has one unusual feature, in that the ratings earned on various play sites play a significant role in deciding the winner.

Full details can be found at http://gamesinmichigan.com/decathlon.

Signup is found at http://gamesinmichigan.com/decathlon2/signup.aspx

You will have to create a gamesinmichigan.com login in order to sign up. I ask for an email address, but it will remain totally private, and only be used for decathlong business. gamesinmichigan.com is a purely hobby site and you won't get any span.

The games to be played, and the sites on which they will be played are:

Bashni- iggamecenter.com
Hex - iggamecenter.com
Toguz Mukalak - iggamecenter.com
Moraboraba - iggamecenter.com
Hive - Boardspace.net
Zertz - Boardspace.net
Lines of Action - Boardspace.net
Xiangqi - playok.com
Shogi - playok.com
Arimaa - Arimaa.com

There are no entry fees, and no prizes. We're doing this because it's fun.
