I was thinking about a mix between Alak and Continuous-Go. The game
is played at a line segment and players drop circular stones with
radius R at any empty space of length 2*R.

- Two stones are connected if their centers have a distance lesser
than 4*R.
- A liberty is an empty segment with at least 2*R.
- Based on these definitions, the GO rules apply.

Variant: stones with different radiuses.

Any thoughts?
