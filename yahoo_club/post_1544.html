New game: 420
http://www.marksteeregames.com/420_rules.pdf

420 is the elusive "Game of X", a generalization of Y in which three players try to connect the four sides of a rhombus with an X shaped path of their own stones, among other winning objectives. Note that the oddness of Y, in particular its threeness, is maintained in 420.

It took me fifteen years to solve the "X problem", but in the end all it took was one more player and a pinch of good old American know how.
