Here are two new games.

Sphinx is a Konane like game because of its patchwork layout and every move must be a capture. It's peculiar movement comes from the Lion, a fairy Chess piece. The rules for Sphinx can be found here:

http://www.zillions-of-games.com/cgi-bin/zilligames/submissions.cgi/88061?do=3Dshow;id=3D1713

Nubia makes uses of a rarely seen form of capture, the withdrawal. The rules for the game can be found here:
