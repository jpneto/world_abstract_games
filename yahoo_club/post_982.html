We need to have a discussion one day about whether games should or
could be capitalized. You know I have written numerous articles for
the Canadian Abstract Games magazine and the editors decided to write
all games (including all old ones) with upper cases. They even had a
discussion about it in the "letters to the editor" section. I doubt
that there are many go books, rather they are Go books. Sackson wrote
all games in upper cases. So he could distinguish Cups (=3Dgame) from
cups (=3Dholes in the game of Cups). But obviously it's possible to
spell games in English as you do. In German, it looks terribly odd -
so if you permit to use the German (original) spelling of my games,
that would be a great favour. --Mancala lover 19:44, 19 August 2006 (CEST)
