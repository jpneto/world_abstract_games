Byte is played with a standard Checkers set. The rules can be found at
bytegame.com. If anyone is interested, I always keep a challenge open
at superdupergames.org.

Impasse is another game for Checkers sets I came out with a couple of
years ago (impasse.net) but which was virtually unknown until it also
appeared on SDG recently. The Impasse instructions are somewhat
lengthy but I'm currently working on a single page rule sheet for it.

Draws and ties cannot occur in either Byte or Impasse. Byte, after
five months of continuous play testing, seems particularly resistant to
first (and second) move advantage - though who knows what the future
may hold.
