Kindle is "Amazon's New Wireless Reading Device", essentially a tablet
computer with a cell phone in it. You get one at Amazon.com, when
they get more in stock.

http://www.amazon.com/Kindle-Amazons-Wireless-Reading-Device/dp/B000FI73MA

Then you buy Kindle ebooks such as Joao Neto's "Mathematical Games,
Abstract Games" from Amazon. The ebook is then downloaded to your
Kindle automatically. There's no need to connect it to a computer.
