Luckily, Byte is adaptable to a variety of Checkers sets. On an 8x8
board with 24 checkers, you form three stacks of eight. On a 10x10
board with 40 checkers, you form five stacks of eight. On a 10x10
board with 30 checkers, you form three stacks of ten. And on a 12x12
board with 60 checkers, you form five stacks of twelve. Basically, if
the number of checkers is evenly divisible by an odd number, you're
good to go.
