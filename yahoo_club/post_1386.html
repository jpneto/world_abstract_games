I think one thing to consider is, can you take a general principle you
are looking to examine and generalize it out into a timeless and
universal abstract concept. I believe you do that, then you are
getting into the realm of being abstract, and an abstract strategy game.

I would count Life as an abstract mathematical model, but definitely
not an abstract strategy game... unless it involved two forces trying
to dominate a play area.
