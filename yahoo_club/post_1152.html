This is to be a recognized list of abstract strategy games by IAGO.
It has grown to try to cover everything abstract strategy out there.
Some games some of the off-wiki sites have a good chance for not
fitting the formal IAGO definitions, but they show a range of what
people consider to be abstract strategy games.
