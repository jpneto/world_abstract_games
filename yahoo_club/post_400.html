Recently I came across an interesting game called Farook but the rules I
have are incomplete so I'm looking for an intact copy. The game is abstract
and attracted my attention for several reasons. Apparently there was a
computer version of Farook that supported seven levels of mastery and the
game publisher, Amuse Inc., offered $1000 to anyone who could beat their
computer at the champion level. The game was also a Mensa select game one
year and was on the Games 100. Admittedly, none of these prove it's a good
game; some of the Mensa select games have been surprisingly mediocre and the
Games 100 selections have often been controversial, but I am curious now to
try the game. If anyone can point me to a copy of the rules I would
appreciate it.
