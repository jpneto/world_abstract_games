I don't know how many of you have noticed Versus. It is designed by
Michel Pinon and you can play it online for free here:

http://www.versus-le-jeu.net/

It will get a limited release probably by the end of April.

Versus uses an interesting concept as your main Versus piece is used
to attract pieces closer to it and change their ownership.

The only problem i noticed after a couple of games online is that the
game may drag near the end.
