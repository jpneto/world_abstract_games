Hi Joao,

Your project looks very interesting! I have a few questions: Is Ludae's purpose to improve the skill of computer players, human players, or both kinds? What is the format of the tournaments, e-mail or online? (E-mail is slower but results in higher quality moves, which would affect how much is really learned). The ELO ratings look to play a crucial role in differentiating "good" knowledge from "bad" knowledge --- but how are the ratings established in the first place? I'm curious about the assigning of discrete values to concepts such as drama and clarity --- is it really so easy? And can a computer be taught to recognize patterns in the same way a human does?
