CERTIFIED HANDMADE IVORY MAMMOTH CHESS-MAN

The gallery has got together the best mammoth bone carvers of the
world, the specialists in history, designers and sculptors.

Every chess collections unique. The job is so complex and unordinary
that some designs are created for even three years.

It's unique not only because it is made of mammoth's bone (an animal
that lived more than 40,000 years ago, and the reserve of the bones of
which seems to exist only in Russia), but because of its cultural and
historic trend.

Designs of chess collections are born in midpoint between the bone
sculpture and Florentine mosaic. So in the "Punic Wars" set, the
Hannibal's "chess army" fights with the Roman legions of Scipion, and
the chess desk looks lire the map of Mediterranean lands.

Collections are produced in small quantity (not more than ten),
quality is strictly controlled. And in every set you can find a
certificate of quality with an individual number of a company register
and a signature and mark of assay Anatoly Karpov, who was the only one
sixteen time's chess champion in the world. The gallery also makes
exclusive chess sets for individual orders, in on exemplar.

The production is absolutely exclusive. It's a symbol of prestige and
prosperity.

Every set is individual and wears an imprint of personal particularity
of the master and the designer.
