Upcoming multi Chess variant tournament on Game Courier

Author: fduniho

Date: 2003-12-19 03:04:34

Post ID: 531

In early 2004, Chessvariants.com will be hosting a multivariant
tournament played entirely on the Game Courier PBM system, located at

http://play.chessvariants.com/pbm/

Game Courier currently supports over 100 Chess variants, as well as
the occasional non-Chess abstract board game, and we are currently
conducting an approval poll to select which of these games will be
considered for the tournament. You may vote in this poll up through
December 31st.

After the first poll narrows down the list, we will conduct a second
poll asking you to rank your preferences. When this poll is over, we
will list the games to be played in the tournament and take some time
to let people sign up. Further details and a link to the poll, which
you can vote in online, are at

http://play.chessvariants.com/tournaments/gamecourier1.html
