IAGO now has a place to list sites that people can play games at! You
can see it here:
http://www.IAGOClubhouse.com

There are over 700 games people can play at these Clubhouse partner
sites right now.

Longer term plans for the Clubhouse include searching by game stored
in a database, so that people can find a particular site that has a
game hey are looking for, and also to store physical locations that
are friendly to having abstract strategy game players. Please view the
site for more details.
