Though I don't have enough experience in Go to
judge but it seems to me another interesting variant,
taking these idea a step forward would be to add to the
usual stones a fix number of stones of life. which
course can be captured like normal stones. But also have
the additional property of giving life to any group
within its reach, that is there exist a path that does
not cross any of the opponent's territory between the
stone of life and your group. Stones of life in this
variant would be two sided so that when captured becomes
a stone of life for the opponent. For a 19x19
board, I would say 4 stones of life per player at the
start of the game.
