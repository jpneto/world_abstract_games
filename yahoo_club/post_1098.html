The bulk of the organization is for average players. What is needed
is to have a critical mass of average players so people can find
opponents to play. This is the idea for the tournament, in order to
get media attention and get average players involved. Also, to
provide average players tools and added benefits to which they would
be able to get some respect for their interest in abstract strategy
games. Things like a membership card that actually enables you to
save money when you go out, and also a return of the magazine Abstract
Games, if not a magazine like it with a different name.

The elites are what you come up with so you end up with a spokesperson
who can speak out and promote abstract strategy games.
