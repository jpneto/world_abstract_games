Chain Reaction
---------------

"If there is no tower on the board, move one of his stones to an
(orthogonal or diagonal) adjacent occupied cell."

No. You can also move to an unoccupied 'cell' and your move ends.

"SCORE - If a player moves a stone inside the initial enemy setup
area, he scores one point."

No. Not 'inside' the set-up area, but behind your opponent's base
line (as in Phutball).

The playing area ic actually 7x8, so the diagram is misleading.

If you are interested in the correct rules:
http://www.ludism.org/misc/chain_reaction/
