Do you want to market it yourself, or submit it to game
publisher? "Out of the Box Publishing" has published some good
abstract games lately. http://otb-games.com/ is their website, and
if you follow the "contact directory" button there's a "product
submissions" button.

Few people get rich marketing abstract games, of course, but I
suppose that's still the best way for a game to become influential
and develop a large following.
