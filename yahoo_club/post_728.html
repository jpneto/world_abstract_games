That is indeed true.

Note that in "padding" the board, you chose this orientation:

v
v v
. V .
V V . h
V H . h h

But you could have chosen this orientation instead:


h h . V .
h V V .
V H .
v v
v

This is *not* the same orientation simply flipped. Yet performing n-1
reduction on this also yields a win for Vertical.

Funny how that works! :)
